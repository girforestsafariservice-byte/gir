Our Story

The Gir Travel Company began with a simple idea: travel Gujarat as kings once did — privately, gracefully, and responsibly. We unite coastal heritage, forest conservation, and desert craft into journeys that feel effortless.

Today, we host travellers from 20+ countries, pairing expert naturalists with trusted drivers, vetted hotels, and meaningful cultural evenings.

Ethics & Conservation

We operate with strict wildlife ethics: no baiting or playback, respectful distances, no flash photography, and guest briefings on park etiquette. A share of our profit supports local initiatives and guides’ training.

Permits & Compliance

Safari permits are arranged end-to-end with full documentation and timing windows. Our processes are licensed and GST-compliant; invoices and vouchers are shared ahead of your arrival.

We maintain emergency response SOPs, vetted transport partners, and verified accommodations with fire & safety compliance.

Sustainability in Action

Low-impact group sizes, refillable water jars, local sourcing, and responsible waste handling. We favour community-run experiences and employ local hosts with fair wages.

FAQs

A quick guide to how we work.

Are you licensed and GST-compliant?

Yes. We operate with valid registrations and provide GST invoices for all billable services.

Do you arrange safari permits?

Yes. We manage end-to-end permits with documented timings and entry zones, subject to park rules.

What are your wildlife ethics?

No flash, no baiting, no playback; respectful distances; capped vehicle numbers; guest briefings before every drive.

Do you support special diets?

Yes—Veg, Non-veg, Jain and allergy-sensitive menus with advance notice.

Is there 24×7 support?

Yes. Our concierge team provides multilingual on-trip support throughout your journey.
