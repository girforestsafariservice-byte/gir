Safari FAQs

How many drives should I plan?

We recommend a minimum of 3–4 drives to experience different zones and animal behaviours across times of day.

Are children allowed?

Yes. We brief families on safety and behaviour, and can suggest gentler routes for younger travellers.

What vehicles do you use?

Government‑approved open jeeps with a trained driver and a naturalist. Private vehicles only—no sharing with other groups.

What about ethical photography?

No baiting, calling, or crowding. We keep respectful distances and follow park rules. Flash is not permitted.

Do you arrange lodging and transfers?

Yes. We run end‑to‑end itineraries with our partner hotels and door‑to‑door logistics.
